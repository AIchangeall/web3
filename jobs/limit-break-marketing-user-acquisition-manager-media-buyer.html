About the Role: We're looking for a User Acquisition Manager to lead and scale our paid and organic growth efforts. You'll sit at the intersection of marketing, data, and product—owning the strategies that bring new users to the platform and keep our growth engine running. This role is ideal for someone who is obsessed with performance metrics and thrives in a fast-paced startup environment. Responsibilities. Entrepreneurial. You're comfortable owning a function with limited oversight and building processes from the ground up. A strong communicator.
