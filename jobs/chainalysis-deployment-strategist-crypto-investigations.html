Global Services is the centre of expertise at Chainalysis — the team that translates our platform, data, and investigative capabilities into real-world impact for customers across law enforcement, national security, financial institutions, and the broader crypto ecosystem. Chainalysis is seeking a Deployment Strategist to join Global Services as a core technical practitioner. This is not a traditional services or solutions engineering role. Deployment Strategists are embedded technical experts who own the full lifecycle of customer value — from problem discovery and investigation through solution design, deployment, and customer enablement. The role sits at the intersection of blockchain investigation, data analytics, domain expertise, and customer partnership. You will work directly with customers to understand their mission, conduct investigations, build tailored solutions, and enable customer teams to operationalise Chainalysis independently. Success requires deep investigative capability, genuine domain expertise, technical skill, and the ability to build trusted relationships through credibility — not just communication. This is the role that makes Chainalysis essential to how our customers operate. In this role, you’ll: - Own end-to-end customer engagements — from discovery through investigation, solution design, deployment, enablement, and ongoing value delivery - Conduct blockchain investigations across the Chainalysis platform, applying tracing, OSINT, and analytical techniques to real customer problems - Partner directly with customers to understand investigative, intelligence, compliance, or operational objectives and translate them into actionable outcomes - Design, build, and maintain custom workflows, notebooks, dashboards, queries, and data models using Chainalysis data and products - Enable customers to operate solutions independently — building their capability so that Chainalysis becomes embedded in their operational infrastructure - Act as the primary technical and domain advisor for your customers, building trusted relationships through hands-on expertise and credibility - Deliver live training, demos, investigative walkthroughs, and technical briefings to both technical and non-technical audiences - Support pre-sales technical engagement for complex and strategic deals — helping customers understand what's possible and how to get there - Collaborate closely with Product, Engineering, Global Intel, and Sales to influence roadmap and improve offerings based on field intelligence - Develop and share domain expertise, investigative techniques, and best practices that raise the capability of the wider GS team We’re looking for candidates who have: - Conducted or directly supported cryptocurrency investigations, blockchain intelligence work, or financial crime analysis in an operational environment - Deep familiarity with blockchain ecosystems (BTC, ETH, stablecoins, DeFi) and how on-chain data is used in investigations, compliance, or intelligence workflows - Delivered customer-facing technical solutions — whether investigative outputs, data products, or analytical workflows — in complex, real-world environments - Worked directly with customers in law enforcement, national security, intelligence, compliance, financial crime, or security-focused domains - Built insights from large or complex datasets and communicated findings to varied audiences — from analysts to executives - Operated successfully in fast-paced, ambiguous environments requiring ownership, adaptability, and sound judgment Required Skills: Technical Skills - Proficiency in Python and SQL for data analysis, transformation, and solution development - Experience building notebooks, dashboards, and analytical workflows - Solid understanding of data modelling, querying, and working with large datasets - Ability to quickly develop deep expertise in Chainalysis data structures, schemas, and products Dom
