职位要求 / 描述

BUILD A SAFER WORLD. TRM Labs provides AI-powered intelligence solutions that help public and private sector agencies investigate and disrupt crime. TRM's platforms enable investigators to trace illicit activity, build cases, and construct operating pictures of threat networks. Leading agencies and businesses worldwide rely on TRM to make the world safer and more secure. The Account Director for Nordic Public Sector will join TRM's team driving our go-to-market strategy across the Nordic region. Their mission is to increase and strengthen TRM's foothold with government agencies, regulators, financial intelligence units, and law enforcement organizations combating financial crime and building resilient digital asset ecosystems. We're looking for a customer-obsessed, consultative salesperson to own the entire sales process with new and existing customers. This person will bring deep knowledge of the cryptocurrency ecosystem and feel comfortable navigating complex Nordic public sector procurement processes to close six- and seven-figure deals. This is a critical role for the EMEA team — an experienced, ready-to-ramp sales professional with executive relationships across Nordic public sector is essential. The impact you will have here: - Account Mapping — Connect with all current key stakeholders within assigned Nordic public sector accounts. Identify what matters to them, their potential use cases, who holds budgets, who makes decisions, who influences decisions, and who owns which process across government agencies, regulators, and law enforcement. - Prospecting — Discover new stakeholders across accounts and identify new agencies and organizations TRM has not yet reached. Secure 8+ discovery meetings across at least 5 distinct Nordic agencies within your first 30 days; complete stakeholder maps and next-steps for each. - Nurturing — Own, plan, execute, and quarterback activities to nurture client relationships, feedback loops, referrals, renewals, upsells, cross-sells, and expansions. Develop long-term strategic partnerships that position TRM as an indispensable mission partner. - Territory Strategy & Planning — Create and execute strategic plans for the Nordics that deliver on key revenue, growth, and retention goals while uncovering new opportunities. Maintain disciplined pipeline coverage and rigorous forecasting. - Product & Subject Matter Expertise — Deepen expertise in TRM's product suite and the Nordic regulatory environment to enrich every stage of the sales process — from demonstrations and trials to customer advisory sessions and innovation workshops. Run at least one innovation workshop per month with a priority agency. - Customer Advocacy — Proactively gather and prioritize customer feedback and champion it within TRM to influence product strategy and roadmap. - Loyalty — Build a portfolio of highly satisfied customers who champion TRM within their networks and across regional forums, refer new prospects, and provide crucial feedback. What we’re looking for: - Experience: 6+ years of B2G SaaS or technology sales experience with a demonstrated ability to consistently deliver against net new sales targets, ideally within public sector, government, or regulatory environments in the Nordic region. - Domain Fluency: Strong understanding of Nordic government workflows, public procurement processes, and the missions of regulatory and enforcement bodies related to financial crime or digital assets. Existing executive-level network across Nordic government agencies is strongly preferred. - Consultative Selling: Strong customer-facing presentation and listening skills with the aptitude to establish credibility with senior government, financial, and compliance stakeholders. Proven ability to shape strategic technology partnerships. - Storytelling & Influence: Natural storyteller with the capacity to understand customer needs and convey compelling value propositions to diverse government audiences.
