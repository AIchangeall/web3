Company Overview Atlas Oracle (www.atlasoracle.io) is a premier on-chain oracle infrastructure provider affiliated with CoinMarketCap (CMC). We bridge the gap between real-world data and smart contracts by providing highly configurable, secure, and reliable data feeds. Unlike traditional oracles that rely on third-party aggregators, Atlas leverages CMC's extensive first-party data network — encompassing over 370 centralized exchanges and 535 decentralized exchanges — to deliver unparalleled accuracy and market coverage. Our core innovation, the ConsensusScore, offers a multi-dimensional assessment of price credibility based on source diversity, price convergence, and manipulation cost. This allows DeFi protocols to implement tiered risk protection, setting a new standard for on-chain security. As we aggressively expand our market share, measured by Total Value Secured (TVS), we are seeking a dynamic leader to drive our marketing and growth initiatives. Role Summary The Head of Marketing & Growth will be a pivotal leader responsible for defining and executing the comprehensive go-to-market strategy for Atlas Oracle. This hybrid role requires a strategic thinker who can seamlessly blend brand marketing, product positioning, and data-driven growth tactics. You will work directly with the CEO to elevate Atlas Oracle's brand presence, clearly articulate our competitive advantages (such as first-party data superiority and the ConsensusScore mechanism), and drive the adoption of our oracle solutions across the DeFi ecosystem. Your primary objective will be to accelerate the growth of our Total Value Secured (TVS) by attracting high-value DeFi protocols, particularly those vulnerable to oracle exploits or those requiring granular configurability. You will orchestrate campaigns that position Atlas Oracle as the most secure, flexible, and reliable alternative to incumbent providers like Chainlink, Pyth, and RedStone Key Responsibilities 1.
