QUALIFICATIONS: - Experience building or optimizing HFT systems, exchange matching engines, or low-latency trading infra. - Strong systems background (Rust, C++, or Go). - Deep understanding of concurrency, networking, and performance profiling. - (Bonus) Experience with blockchains, consensus, or distributed systems. WHAT WE OFFER: - Competitive salary. - High-impact role in an ambitious, fast-moving startup. - Travel opportunities and thought leadership visibility in the crypto community. Our team is creating a chainless, blockless, leaderless protocol that challenges the conventional notion of consensus.
