ABOUT POLYGON LABS Polygon Labs is a global blockchain payments company building and operating infrastructure to move money instantly, reliably, and at internet scale, with the mission to move all money onchain. It is building the Polygon Open Money Stack, an open and integrated stack of services and technologies to instantly and reliably move money anywhere, and put it to work. Its infrastructure has facilitated trillions of dollars in onchain value transfer and supported millions of transactions daily for some of the globe's largest banks, fintechs, enterprises, and consumer applications. YOUR ROLE Do you want to architect and build a modern payments platform from the ground up, not just manage it? As Tech Lead, Payments at Polygon Labs, you will be the hands-on technical driver behind the Open Money Stack and On-Chain Money vision. This is a deeply technical role: you will spend the majority of your time writing production-grade Go, designing core services, and making foundational architecture decisions that power fiat and blockchain-based payment flows at scale. This is a greenfield opportunity with startup intensity, high ownership, high velocity, and the ability to shape systems before they solidify. You will act as the technical authority for Payments, setting engineering standards, reviewing designs, and guiding other engineers through complex distributed systems challenges, while remaining deeply embedded in the codebase. You will operate as an AI-native tech lead, using coding agents and AI tooling daily to ship at a velocity and scope that would traditionally require a larger team, and you will be expected to set the bar for how the Payments org adopts and compounds AI in its engineering workflow. If you thrive on building mission-critical financial infrastructure, care about clean system design, and want to help move global payments onchain, this role is built for you. Define service boundaries, data models, and integration contracts across synchronous and asynchronous systems - Establish engineering standards for reliability, observability, security, and performance across the Payments stack -
