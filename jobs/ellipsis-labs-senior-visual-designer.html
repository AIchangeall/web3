职位要求 / 描述

Ellipsis Labs is a profitable, venture-backed New York-based startup building sustainable and efficient DeFi protocols on high-throughput decentralized infrastructure. Our long-term goal is to build a better financial system. Our flagship product, Phoenix Perpetuals, brings professional-grade perpetual futures to Solana. Building on our experience developing efficient markets, Ellipsis Labs also operates SolFi, a proprietary automated market maker that provides efficient liquidity for key trading pairs. Our foundational product, Phoenix Legacy, is a limit order book on the Solana blockchain that established our expertise in operating high-performance markets. Combined, these products have facilitated over $285B in trading volume across Solana markets. Ellipsis Labs is seeking a Senior Designer to define and elevate the visual language of our exchange and brand across high-density, real-time trading interfaces. You’ll partner with engineering and leadership to craft a cohesive, trustworthy look and feel - from component libraries to data visualization, motion, and launch assets. In this role, you will: - Own the visual system for Phoenix and future products: typography, color, spacing, iconography, design tokens, and component theming - Turn complex trading mechanics and data into clear, scannable visual hierarchies and data visualizations that perform under real-time constraints - Build and maintain a Figma library/design system, ensuring consistency and speed of implementation; provide developer-ready specs and assets - Drive visual polish on critical surfaces (order entry, positions, charts, risk states), including loading/empty/error states and micro-interactions/motion - Partner closely with engineers to ensure visual fidelity in production and pragmatic choices under technical constraints - Own the company and product brand expression across product surfaces and key moments - Incorporate feedback, user signals, and metrics to iterate quickly without sacrificing craft QUALIFICATIONS Required - 2+ years designing visual systems for complex digital products, ideally high-density or data-heavy (trading/finance, analytics, dev tools, etc.). - Portfolio demonstrating strong visual craft (type, color, layout), system thinking (tokens/components), and clear data presentation. - Fluency in Figma (components/variants, auto-layout) and handoff practices; attention to detail across states and edge cases. - Comfort collaborating with engineers; ability to balance visual quality with performance and feasibility. - High ownership and bias to ship; you sweat the details and move quickly. Preferred - Familiarity with crypto/Web3 design patterns and user behaviors - Exposure to Solana or similar blockchain ecosystems
