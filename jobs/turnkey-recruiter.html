职位要求 / 描述

YOUR ROLE As a Recruiter at Turnkey, you’ll own the full recruiting lifecycle - from sourcing and outreach through to offer and close - across technical and business roles. You’ll partner closely with hiring managers to ensure smooth, fast-moving processes, deliver an exceptional candidate experience, and raise the bar for every hire we make. You’ll also be a builder: refining our systems, strengthening our employer brand, and creating the recruiting foundation that will scale with the company as we continue our rapid growth. This is a high-impact, high-visibility role ideal for someone who thrives in fast-paced environments, loves designing systems, and takes pride in running exceptionally tight processes. WHAT YOU’LL DO - Manage searches end-to-end: source, screen, interview, and close top candidates across multiple departments - Build trusted relationships with hiring managers to deeply understand role needs and ensure alignment throughout the process - Source creatively and strategically to identify talent beyond traditional channels - Own candidate experience from first touch through close, ensuring every interaction reflects Turnkey’s standards of excellence - Maintain organized, high-quality data and reporting within our ATS - Partner with leadership to forecast hiring needs and define hiring plans - Strengthen Turnkey’s employer brand through thoughtful storytelling and consistent messaging - Continuously refine recruiting processes to increase efficiency, speed, and quality of hire - Experiment, measure, and improve; help Turnkey develop a recruiting engine built to scale WHAT WE’RE LOOKING FOR - 5+ years of full-cycle recruiting experience, ideally within a high-growth technology company - Proven track record of successfully closing candidates across technical and business functions - Highly organized and systems-oriented; you naturally bring structure to fast-moving environments - Comfortable managing multiple roles at once without sacrificing quality or attention to detail - Excellent written and verbal communication skills - Relentlessly hardworking, resourceful, and committed to finding and winning the best talent - Strong judgment and ability to represent Turnkey with professionalism and warmth - Deep interest in startups, technology, and helping great people find meaningful work STYLE POINTS - Existing network within fintech, crypto, or developer-focused companies - Strong understanding of compensation benchmarking and offer strategy - Experience implementing or improving ATS workflows, specifically with Ashby - A natural storyteller who can convey Turnkey’s mission and values authentically WHAT WE OFFER - Full benefits, including medical, dental, vision, life, disability, HSA/FSA, 401(k) — detailed benefits overview available as we get further in the process - Paid parental leave - Unlimited PTO (and we will force you to take time off!) - $3,000/yr learning and development budget to attend industry conferences - Multiple team offsites per year - Macbook Pro laptop - Lunch stipend Ready to join us on this journey?
