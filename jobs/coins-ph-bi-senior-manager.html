Join the Pioneer Crypto Brand in the Philippines! Coins is the most established crypto brand in The Philippines and has gained the trust of more than 18 million users. You will lead the team to build and optimize the BI system, integrate data from digital currency trading, payment services, user behavior, risk control, and compliance, and provide accurate, timely, and actionable data insights for the company’s senior management, business departments, and compliance teams. This role requires deep understanding of the crypto and payment industry, strong data analysis capabilities, and excellent team management skills to align BI work with the company’s business development, risk control, and compliance goals.
