Preferred Qualifications • AWS/Azure/GCP cloud certification (e.g., AWS Data Analytics Specialty). • Experience in building data middle platforms and enterprise-level data assetization. • Familiar with financial/crypto data compliance requirements (GDPR, local financial regulations). • Hands-on experience with DevOps/Infra-as-Code tools (Terraform) and BI tools (Metabase).
