Join the Pioneer Crypto Brand in the Philippines! Coins is the most established crypto brand in The Philippines and has gained the trust of more than 18 million users. Responsibilities： • Cross-Border Bank Account ManagementLead the process of opening, maintaining, and closing bank accounts globally in coordination with business units and finance teams. Ensure all accounts are compliant with local regulatory and internal policy requirements. • Banking Relationship ManagementBuild and maintain strong relationships with local and international banking partners to support operational, investment, and strategic financial needs. Negotiate favorable terms, fees, and service levels with banking partners. Act as the primary liaison for all global banking partners and financial institutions. • Strategic Treasury OperationsSupport the business in identifying optimal banking solutions in each jurisdiction based on transactional, regulatory, and risk considerations. Monitor global cash flow movements, intercompany transfers, and funding requirements. • Risk, Compliance & DocumentationEnsure treasury practices meet regulatory, KYC, and AML requirements in each operating country. Maintain up-to-date documentation and control processes for all bank accounts and related mandates. • Collaboration & Expansion SupportCollaborate with business units, legal, tax, and compliance teams to support expansion into new markets with appropriate banking infrastructure. Provide treasury expertise in M&A activities, new product launches, or country entries. • Technology & ReportingSupport the implementation and optimization of treasury systems and automation tools. Prepare regular reports on bank account usage, balances, liquidity, and treasury KPIs for senior leadership. Exposure management: Manage crypto and foreign currency exposure and payments, forecasting of medium and long- term funding and hedging requirements to deploy proper risk management and risk mitigation strategies.
