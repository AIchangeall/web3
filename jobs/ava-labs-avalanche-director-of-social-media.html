Director of Social Media Location: East Coast (NYC Preferred) We are looking for an exceptional Director of Social Media to lead Avalanche’s brand presence across all owned channels and shape how the world perceives Avalanche. This role requires someone who is highly creative, self-sufficient with production tools, and passionate about building in public. You will own our voice and move fast.
