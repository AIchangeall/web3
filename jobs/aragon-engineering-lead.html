As a cross-functional Engineering Lead on Aragon's Platform Team, you'll code and coordinate across frontend, backend, and Aragon OSx smart contract framework, owning some of the most challenging features, architecture cohesion, operational discipline, and platform reliability. Responsibilities: - Own challenging features and architecture cohesion across the full stack - Strengthen the team by mentoring engineers at every level - Document critical knowledge and reference material - Code alongside the team — frontend, backend, or contracts — and review peers' work - Drive operational discipline and platform reliability Requirements: - Senior-level depth in either frontend (JS/TS and a modern framework, ideally React) or backend (Node/TS or similar) or both - CI/CD literacy (e.g., GitHub Actions, CircleCI) and practical understanding of monitoring/alerts and incident handling - Experience working on open source projects ideally in blockchain, cryptography, smart contracts, or web3 - Experience as an engineering manager, tech lead, or similar technical leadership role, ideally for a remote team Aragon is a remote distributed organization that's flexible as to where you want to work and your schedule.
