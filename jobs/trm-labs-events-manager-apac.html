BUILD A SAFER WORLD. TRM Labs provides AI-powered intelligence solutions that help public and private sector agencies investigate and disrupt crime. As the Events Manager APAC, you will partner closely with Marketing, Policy, and Sales to drive event-led pipeline growth across the region. You will own the end-to-end strategy and execution of sponsored, co-hosted, and TRM-hosted events and webinars, while building scalable operational processes that increase efficiency, consistency, and measurable impact. This role requires a balance of strategic thinking and hands-on execution. You will ensure every event is aligned to commercial objectives, delivers quantifiable business outcomes, and continuously strengthens TRM’s regional events engine.
