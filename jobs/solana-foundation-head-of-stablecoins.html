职位要求 / 描述

WHO WE ARE The Solana Foundation is a non-profit based in Zug, Switzerland, dedicated to the adoption, decentralization, and security of the Solana network. Solana is a high performance blockchain that can deliver a fast and friendly user experience, without sacrificing security. The Solana Foundation is working to realize a world where individuals own their data, use permissionless networks, and transfer information and value freely around the world. We are looking for talented people who are willing to jump right in and use their expertise to help the ecosystem build. The Opportunity Solana Foundation is seeking a Head of Stablecoins to lead the strategy, growth, and ecosystem development of stablecoins on Solana. This is not a traditional BD or product role. The right person will own the full picture — from forging partnerships with stablecoin issuers, exchanges, and DeFi protocols, to designing strategies that drive meaningful liquidity onto Solana, to working with product and engineering teams to remove friction and unlock new use cases. The ideal candidate thinks like a founder: they can set a vision, build relationships at the highest levels, get deep into mechanism design and tokenomics, and execute relentlessly across multiple work-streams simultaneously. They understand that growing stablecoins on Solana requires a combination of strategic partnerships, smart commercial structures, product thinking, and ecosystem coordination. The mandate of this role is not to drive organic or marginal growth, rather it is to drive step function/breakthrough growth. What You'll Do - Define and own Solana Foundation's stablecoin growth strategy end-to-end — from issuer relationships to on-chain distribution and utility - Build and manage relationships with major stablecoin issuers, exchanges, custodians, market makers, and DeFi protocols to deepen stablecoin supply and usage on Solana - Design and oversee commercial strategies, and liquidity initiatives that attract and retain stablecoin capital on-chain - Work closely with product and engineering teams to identify infrastructure gaps, improve developer tooling, and enable new stablecoin use cases (payments, treasury, remittances, DeFi) - Collaborate with ecosystem teams across DeFi, payments, and institutional adoption to ensure stablecoin growth compounds across verticals - Track competitive dynamics across chains, analyze what's working, and adapt strategy quickly - Represent Solana Foundation in executive conversations, industry working groups, and at major conferences What We're Looking For - 7+ years of experience spanning some combination of business development, product strategy, growth, or protocol/ecosystem leadership — ideally in crypto or fintech - A founder's mindset: high agency, comfortable with ambiguity, able to context-switch between a partnership negotiation, an incentive model, and a product spec in the same day - Strong understanding of stablecoin mechanics, DeFi liquidity dynamics, and on-chain incentive design - Proven ability to build and maintain senior relationships with issuers, protocols, and institutional partners - Sharp analytical thinking — comfortable working with data, modeling scenarios, and making trade-offs - Excellent communicator who can translate complex on-chain dynamics into clear strategic narratives for both crypto-native and traditional audiences - Comfortable operating autonomously in a fast-moving, lean, globally distributed team Bonus Points - Direct experience at a stablecoin issuer, DeFi protocol, L1/L2, or crypto-native fund - Background in commercial strategies, tokenomics, or liquidity strategy - Existing relationships with major stablecoin issuers and key DeFi teams building on Solana or across chains - Experience in payments infrastructure or traditional capital markets
