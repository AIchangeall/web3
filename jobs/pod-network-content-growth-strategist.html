ABOUT THE ROLE We're seeking a content-driven, audience-obsessed marketer to help tell the story of pod.network http://pod.network. You'll be responsible for building and growing our audience across various channels — from creating viral X threads to producing engaging content, including blogs, documentation, videos, and memes. If you love shaping narratives, have a knack for going viral, and thrive at the intersection of storytelling and data, this role is for you. YOU'LL BE A GREAT FIT IF YOU - Are a crisp, clear writer who can simplify complex technical concepts. - Can quickly grasp deep tech ideas and turn them into digestible content. - Know how to make things go viral — and have examples to prove it. - Are terminally online (or open to it) — especially on Telegram and X. - Self-starter mindset with a feel for community culture (e.g. Crypto Twitter). - Have a data-driven mindset and know how to measure what works. BONUS POINTS IF YOU - Bring a portfolio of high-performing crypto or Web3 content (memes, threads, videos, etc.). - Have stage presence and are excited about public speaking opportunities (podcasts, panels, etc.). - Understand blockchain scaling and cryptographic protocols. - Have experience writing developer or research-oriented technical documentation. WHAT WE OFFER - Competitive salary. - A front-row seat to a groundbreaking layer-1 protocol, reshaping how blockchains work. - Creative freedom and influence in a fast-paced, ambitious startup. - Travel opportunities and thought leadership visibility in the crypto community.
