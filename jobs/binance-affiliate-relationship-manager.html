职位要求 / 描述

Binance is a leading global blockchain ecosystem behind the world’s largest cryptocurrency exchange by trading volume and registered users. We are trusted by 300+ million people in 100+ countries for our industry-leading security, user fund transparency, trading engine speed, deep liquidity, and an unmatched portfolio of digital-asset products. Binance offerings range from trading and finance to education, research, payments, institutional services, Web3 features, and more. We leverage the power of digital assets and blockchain to build an inclusive financial ecosystem to advance the freedom of money and improve financial access for people around the world. About the Role We are looking for an experienced Affiliate Relationship Manager (ARM) to manage and grow long-term relationships with key affiliates and channel partners across global markets. This role is not focused on cold acquisition or aggressive business development. Instead, the ARM acts as a strategic partner manager responsible for affiliate retention, revenue growth, relationship stability, and risk awareness. The ideal candidate understands the crypto/FX affiliate ecosystem deeply, is highly relationship-oriented, commercially sensitive, and capable of balancing growth with compliance and platform interests. trading volume, retention, and revenue contribution • Monitor affiliate performance metrics and identify abnormal behaviors, risks, or growth opportunities • Handle affiliate concerns professionally, execute joint campaigns, incentive programs, and partnership initiatives with affiliates • Analyze affiliate ROI, trading behaviors, and lifecycle performance to improve overall ecosystem efficiency • Identify and prevent potential risks such as wash trading, arbitrage abuse, multi-accounting, or non-compliant traffic sources • Maintain strong market awareness of competitor affiliate programs, commission structures, and industry trends • Support affiliate onboarding, due diligence, and long-term relationship development Requirements:： Industry Experience • Experience in Crypto, FX, CFD,
