1inch is the DeFi ecosystem building financial freedom for everyone. 1inch products help users and builders trade, hold and track digital assets - with the self-custody, comprehensive security and the intuitive user experience they need to unlock the potential of true crypto ownership. 1inch protocols and APIs provide core infrastructure across the DeFi industry and beyond.   So if you’re someone who thinks big, moves fast and wants to make an impact right from day one, then get ready to join our industry-changing team.   Location: We prioritise candidates who are based in Dubai then consider those working remotely in one of our European Hub Locations (UK, Spain or Germany).   The Senior Technical Product Manager, Swap owns the end-to-end success of the Swap product domain. This role combines strong product leadership, technical understanding, and market awareness to ensure the Swap product remains competitive, reliable, scalable, and valuable for users and partners. The role reports to the Head of Product, B2B SaaS and heavily collaborates with Product Owners to connect product strategy with daily execution across teams. Within the Product department, the person in this role works closely with Product Design, QA, and Engineering teams, while partnering cross-functionally with Marketing, Legal, and Sales & Business Development to align priorities and drive measurable outcomes. This role requires a product manager who can understand complex DeFi and technical concepts, translate them into clear product decisions, and drive the right actions to maintain or improve the product’s competitive position.
