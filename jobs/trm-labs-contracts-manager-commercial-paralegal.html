TRM's platforms enable investigators to trace illicit activity, build cases, and construct operating pictures of threat networks. Leading agencies and businesses worldwide rely on TRM to make the world safer and more secure. As a Commercial Paralegal (AI & Legal Operations), you will report to the Assistant General Counsel – Commercial and join a high-trust, high-velocity Legal team that supports TRM's global Go-To-Market (GTM) and vendor functions. You'll be equal parts legal operations problem-solver and AI-native workflow builder, owning the workstreams that keep deals moving — MNDAs, order forms, intake triage, and signature routing — and turning them into repeatable, AI-assisted processes instead of one-off fire drills. Success looks like a legal team that focuses its time on truly complex matters because the rest of the work runs through well-designed agentic systems, with measurable improvements in cycle time, throughput, and consistency. Please note: there is a strong preference for Eastern or Central time zones to stay close to the core legal team.
