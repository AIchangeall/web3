About us Bitget Wallet is the non-custodial crypto wallet designed to make crypto simple and secure for everyone. With over 80 million users, we bring together a full suite of crypto services, including swaps, market insights, staking, rewards, DApp exploration, and payment solutions. Supporting 130+ blockchains and millions of tokens, Bitget Wallet enables seamless multi-chain trading across hundreds of DEXs and cross-chain bridges. Backed by a $300+ million user protection fund, it ensures the highest level of security for users' assets. What you'll do • Design and develop brand assets, including logos, typography, color systems, and visual guidelines that reinforce a consistent brand identity. • Create marketing and communication materials such as banners, social media creatives, pitch decks, infographics, event collateral, and campaign visuals. • Collaborate closely with marketing, product, and content teams to deliver design solutions that engage users and align with business objectives. • Translate complex ideas—especially within cryptocurrency, Web3, and digital finance—into clear, engaging, and visually appealing graphics. • Maintain high standards of visual design and ensure accuracy, consistency, and attention to detail across all creative outputs. • Use design tools (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Figma) to produce high-quality deliverables optimized for both digital and print usage. • Research design trends and emerging creative approaches, bringing fresh and innovative ideas into projects. • Manage multiple design requests in a fast-moving environment while meeting deadlines. What you'll need • Bachelor’s degree or above in design or related field, with 3+ years of brand design experience; • Proficient in design tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Figma; • Strong aesthetic sense and excellent color control ability; • Able to adapt to a fast-paced and remote team work environment; • Passionate about cryptocurrency, Web3, and the future of digital finance. • Experience in advertising agencies and strong English skills are preferred. 工作内容 • 设计和开发品牌资产，包括标志、字体系统、色彩体系和视觉指南，强化一致的品牌形象。 • 制作营销与传播材料，如横幅、社交媒体创意、路演文稿、信息图表、活动物料及市场活动视觉设计。 • 与市场、产品和内容团队紧密合作，提供能够吸引用户且符合业务目标的设计方案。 • 将复杂概念——尤其是加密货币、Web3 和数字金融相关内容——转化为清晰、有吸引力且具视觉感染力的图形。 • 保持高标准的视觉设计，确保所有创意产出在准确性、一致性和细节上的高水准。 • 使用设计工具（Photoshop、Illustrator、InDesign、Figma）制作高质量的交付物，优化适配数字与印刷场景。 • 研究设计趋势与新兴创意方法，为项目带来新颖且创新的想法。 • 在快节奏的环境下同时管理多个设计需求，并按时完成。 岗位要求 • 设计或相关专业本科及以上学历，3 年以上品牌设计经验； • 熟练掌握 Photoshop、Illustrator、InDesign 和 Figma 等设计工具； • 具有较强的审美能力和优秀的色彩把控能力； • 能适应快节奏及远程团队的工作环境； • 对加密货币、Web3 及数字金融的未来充满热情； • 有广告公司经验及较强英文能力者优先。 If you are ambitious and believe that digital assets could be the next financial and technological revolution, please apply!
