职位要求 / 描述

As a Staff Product Marketing Manager you will lead high-impact go-to-market strategy and execution for some of our most important products and growth initiatives. You’ll own positioning, messaging, and launch end-to-end, build scalable frameworks for how we bring products to market, and act as a senior thought partner to product and business leaders. This is a highly visible role for someone with proven success at a top-tier B2B SaaS company. The right candidate will be ready to operate at a fast-pace incorporating new AI solutions within our category-defining blockchain data analytics company. In this role, you’ll: - First and foremost, design and refine messaging frameworks that clearly articulate customer problems, our solution, and why we win vs. key competitors - Own GTM strategy for a major product area: define target segments, use cases, and differentiated positioning, and translate them into clear narratives and offers for the field - Lead major product launches and cross-functional initiatives from concept through post-launch impact, aligning Product, Sales, Marketing, Customer Success, and Partnerships on a single plan - Develop deep customer and market insight by partnering with product, customers, sales, and data teams to inform roadmap, positioning, and opportunity sizing - Drive competitive intelligence for your area, turning noisy inputs into concise insights and guidance that influence product strategy and front-line execution - Serve as an executive-ready storyteller, representing your product area in internal reviews, field enablement sessions, customer meetings, and external-facing content - Mentor other PMMs and cross-functional partners, modeling high standards for craft, influence, and collaboration in a C7 “senior staff” capacity We’re looking for candidates who have: - 8+ years of product marketing experience in SaaS, including ownership of GTM for complex, multi-stakeholder products - Proven work history at a top-tier SaaS company or similar rigorous environment that demonstrates impact and speed to market - A track record of leading high-impact launches end-to-end (strategy, positioning, packaging, enablement, and measurement) with clear business results - Exceptional storytelling and communication skills — you can distill complexity into crisp narratives for executives, customers, and field teams, and are a strong presenter - Depth in revenue enablement, including building and rolling out pitch decks, playbooks, and competitive materials that change how teams sell - Demonstrated impact on product and business strategy, using customer, competitive, and market insights to influence roadmap and investment decisions - Experience partnering closely with Sales and CS leadership to align on pipeline, win-rate, and adoption goals and build plans to achieve them - Comfort operating at senior-staff level (C7): working autonomously, leading cross-functional programs, and driving alignment across senior stakeholders Nice to have experience: - Background in B2B SaaS, risk & compliance, or data/analytics products - Successful implementation of AI automated workflows for marketing content - Experience in highly regulated or enterprise environments (financial services, public sector, or cybersecurity) - Prior work on pricing & packaging, segmentation, or portfolio strategy - Experience with crypto, web3, or blockchain analytics—or strong interest in becoming an expert in this space - Experience managing or mentoring PMMs, or acting as a craft lead for a PMM pod Technologies we use and teach: - AI agents with custom harnessing - Sales and marketing stacks such as Salesforce, Seismic/KNR, Marketo, and common analytics tools - Crypto, web3 security, and risk/compliance concepts (we’ll help you ramp here) Please note: This role requires in-person collaboration in either NYC or SF office.
