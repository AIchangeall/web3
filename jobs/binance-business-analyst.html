Binance is a leading global blockchain ecosystem behind the world’s largest cryptocurrency exchange by trading volume and registered users. Qualifications： • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Business, Statistics, or related field.市场营销、商业、统计学或相关专业本科及以上学历. • Proven experience in marketing analysis, business intelligence, or related roles.具备市场分析、商业智能或相关岗位的工作经验. • Proficiency in data analysis tools such as Excel, SQL, Tableau, Power BI, or similar.熟练使用Excel、SQL、Tableau、Power BI等数据分析工具. • Strong analytical skills with the ability to interpret complex data sets.较强的分析能力，能够解读复杂数据. • Bilingual English/Mandarin is required to be able to coordinate with overseas partners and stakeholders.
