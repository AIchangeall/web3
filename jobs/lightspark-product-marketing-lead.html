Lightspark is creating the Money Grid, modern, open financial infrastructure that makes payments accessible, affordable, and instant for businesses and users worldwide. We're challenging the status quo, updating the current system, and empowering our partners to build better solutions. Together, we're building the most advanced financial infrastructure, open, instant, and global, inspiring a future where anyone, anywhere can access the full potential of digital money. We're looking for a senior Product Marketing Lead to give every prospect every reason to say yes. You sit at the intersection of product, sales, and the market translating what we build into why it matters. You own positioning across our product lines, arm the sales team with the tools to win, and make our website work as hard as we do. This is a high-impact, high-visibility role that shapes how the world understands Lightspark. WHAT YOU'LL BE DOING - Develop sharp, differentiated positioning for Grid and Spark. You know our buyers, their pain points, and exactly why Lightspark wins. You make sure everyone from sales to CEO tells the same compelling story. - Own go-to-market strategy for partner launches. Build the playbooks, coordinate cross-functional teams, and make every launch land. - Lead and continuously evolve our Demo Friday program. Make sure our demos are compelling, current, and closing. - Create and maintain the decks, one-pagers, battlecards, and objection-handling guides that empower our sales team to move faster and close bigger deals. - Build a library of customer stories that make prospects see themselves in our wins. Work closely with customers and the customer success team to capture and tell these stories powerfully. - Own the product and solution pages on lightspark.com http://lightspark.com. Every page should answer a buyer's question and move them forward. Work with design and engineering to keep the site sharp and conversion-focused. - Stay close to the market. Know the competitive landscape, track how it shifts, and make sure our positioning stays ahead. WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR - 7+ years of product marketing experience, with at least 2 years at a senior level in B2B SaaS, fintech, or enterprise infrastructure - Proven ability to develop product positioning and messaging that resonates with enterprise buyers and technical audiences - Experience owning go-to-market launches end to end, including partner or channel launches - Proven fluency with AI tools you use them to move faster, write better, and experiment smarter - Exceptional written and verbal communication you can write a crisp exec deck and a detailed competitive teardown with equal ease - Strong cross-functional collaborator: you know how to work with Product, Sales, Design, and Leadership to align and execute - Data-driven: you measure what matters and use it to sharpen your approach - A high bar for quality and a restless drive to make everything better Lightspark is on a mission to build the future of open, instant, and global financial infrastructure making payments accessible and affordable for everyone, everywhere.
