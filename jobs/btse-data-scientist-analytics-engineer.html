Responsibilities:： • Build dashboards • Build, iterate on, and maintain dashboards • Take dashboards through the full lifecycle: requirements gathering, prototyping, build, review, deployment, and ongoing maintenance Learn the data • Develop a solid working knowledge of the underlying datasets that power the business — what is in them, how they connect, where the pitfalls are • Write clean, well-documented SQL and Python to extract, transform, and analyse data • Ask good questions: about the data, about the business, about whether a metric is actually measuring what it claims to measure Support the team • Pick up smaller analyses, ad-hoc requests, and follow-up work to keep the team moving • Document your work clearly so others can build on it • Grow into more complex projects over time as your skills develop Qualifications:： • You are proficient in Python and SQL • Solid working SQL — joins, aggregations, CTEs, window functions; able to write queries that other people can read and trust • Comfortable in Python for data work — pandas at minimum, with willingness to learn PySpark and the broader stack • Some exposure to modern data tools (Databricks, dbt, Tableau, Looker, or similar) is a plus — but more important is the willingness to learn You have a good feel for data • Some experience working with real datasets — through internships, projects, coursework, or a previous role • You enjoy digging into data to understand what is going on; you are not satisfied with a query that runs but produces a number you cannot explain • Basic instincts for data quality — you notice when something looks off Perks & Benefits:： • Competitive salary and benefits package. • Opportunity to work in a fast-paced and innovative environment. • Be part of a growing and dynamic team. • Make a real impact on the company's success. • Various team building programs and company events. • Comprehensive healthcare schemes for employees and dependants. • And many more!
