About us Bitget is one of the world's leading digital assets ecosystems. With over 120 million registered users, Bitget has one of the most comprehensive suites of blockchain products and services available via bitget.com. Our mission is to support the growth of the digital assets industry and we believe it represents the future of finance. What we do empowers the future of finance by ensuring secure, efficient and smart digital transactions. We are one of the fastest growing companies in the digital asset sector. If you are looking for cutting-edge work, where you will have opportunities to develop your career among peers who are experts in their field, and you believe in the future of digital currency, then look no further than Bitget! What you'll do Institutional & VIP Trading Solutions • Provide trading/execution solutions for VIP, professional and institutional clients: product/contract setup, leverage & margin, trading rules, execution path and slippage explanation, liquidity/routing recommendations, etc. • Partner with Sales/Channels/Client teams to clarify institutional requirements, support onboarding, and propose implementable setups (LP mix, routing/hedging, markups/rebates, risk limits and operating workflows). • Collect and analyze VIP/institutional feedback on spreads, fill rates, slippage and platform experience; drive internal improvements with clear ownership and closure. Liquidity & Execution Management • Coordinate with LPs, validate pricing quality and stability, and monitor execution KPIs (spreads, slippage, rejects/requotes, fill rates, latency, outliers). • Maintain LP scorecards (by symbol/time/volatility regimes) and drive improvements in LP performance and routing decisions. • Maintain and optimize A/B book or STP/ECN execution, routing and hedging policies aligned with risk and compliance requirements. MT5 & Bridge/Aggregator Operations • Operate MT5 and bridge/aggregators: health checks, parameter changes, upgrades, UAT, rollback planning and change management. • Configure MT5 groups/symbols, margin & leverage, commissions & swaps, markups/rebates, routing/hedging rules and risk limits. • Troubleshoot the execution chain via logs/reports: order anomalies, pricing sessions, routing, FIX connectivity, latency and packet loss; drive resolution with IT/vendors. Trade Support & Operational Controls • Handle MT5 tickets on order status, rejections/slippage, margin/leverage, PnL/swaps/commissions and platform issues with clear findings, evidence and fixes. • Perform daily reconciliations and operational controls (trades/positions/balances/fees) and produce T+0/T+1 reports. Risk & Compliance Liaison • Interpret trading/risk reports, monitor net exposure, concentrations, NOP and suspicious activities; enforce SOPs and audit trails. • Produce risk alerts and concise market briefs during high volatility; provide trading-side explanations to Business/Sales/Support. Documentation & Continuous Improvement • Maintain runbooks/SOPs, conduct post-incident reviews, enhance monitoring/KPIs and drive continuous process/system improvements. What you'll need • Preferred experience includes one or more of the following: • CFD Liquidity Provider (LP): Trading Ops / Risk / Execution / Coverage; or • CFD Broker: dealing, trading ops, risk, execution; or • CFD infrastructure vendor: MT5/bridge/aggregator implementation & operations; • Institutional-facing BD/solutioning experience is a strong plus. • Strong knowledge of institutional execution and risk concepts: STP/ECN, A/B book, routing/hedging, institutional liquidity & pricing structure, leverage/margin, NOP/net exposure management. • Experience working with brokers, IBs, LPs, prime/bridge vendors; capable of delivering professional service and actionable solutions. • Excellent communication and presentation skills; able to explain execution quality, trading rules and incident root causes in a structured manner and drive cross-team delivery. •
