As a Software Engineer in the Investigations group, you'll join an engineering team in Aarhus, Denmark, working together to build high quality customer experiences. This role involves leading the development of customer-facing web frontend features alongside the supporting backend services, requiring you to deeply understand the underlying data platform to effectively partner with product managers and designers in delivering impactful solutions. The mission of the Graphs team is to enable amazing graphs for crypto investigators, and to make it easy for other teams to deliver valuable customer insights related to crypto investigations. Often crime investigators stumble upon crypto-related evidence and need to understand whether it is linked to a bigger crypto scheme. Investigators achieve this by graphing out crypto entities to follow the money and/or change of hands in pursuit of identifying the perpetrators. We help investigators by grouping addresses into a bunch of provably coherent clusters and then categorize the clusters with additional insights, e.g. naming a gambling service. We do this with algorithms powered by verifiable facts, but also offer signals based on probability-based algorithms like machine learning. In this role, you’ll: - Grow to be part of an established team with a smooth and sustainable way of collaborating and sharing tasks. A team which can focus on generating impact and spend less time on maintenance and bugs. - Be working and pairing with other members of your dedicated team, becoming a key contributor to solutions with a high degree of availability and low maintainability. - Be able to take great pride in collaborating on and materializing product ideas through production in the frontend.
